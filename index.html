Chapter 14: Types of Houses

Houses protect us from rain, heat, cold, wind, wild animals, and thieves. Different types are found in villages and cities.

🏠
📌 Key fact: A house is a place where people live with their families. In India, houses range from ancient havelis to modern multi-storey towers!

Chapter 18: Water Sources

Earth is the Blue Planet — about two-thirds of it is covered with water. We get water from rain, rivers, lakes, groundwater, and oceans.

💧
🌍 Did you know? Earth is the only planet that has water in liquid form. Water covers about 71% of Earth's surface — that's why astronauts call it the Blue Planet!

Water Sources Cards
🌧️
Rain
Main source of freshwater. Differs by season & location.
🪣
Groundwater
Rain seeps underground. Drawn via wells & handpumps.
🔵
Pond
Small water body surrounded by land. May dry in summer.
🏞️
Lake
Larger than a pond. Surrounded by land on all sides.
🌊
River
Natural flow across land. Starts from glaciers or springs.
🌍
Ocean / Sea
5 oceans on Earth. Salty water — cannot drink directly.
🏗️
Dam
Wall across river. Stores water & generates electricity.
🏞️
Reservoir
Artificial lake behind a dam. Stores blocked river water.
〰️
Canal
Channel carrying water from river/reservoir to fields.
🔥
Evaporation
Water → water vapour due to heat from the sun.
❄️
Condensation
Water vapour → water due to cooling.
🧊
Glacier
Large rivers of ice in mountains. Source of snow-fed rivers.
🇮🇳 India's Rivers: Snow-fed rivers (Ganga, Yamuna, Brahmaputra) — source in Himalayas, have water year-round. Rain-fed rivers (Godavari, Krishna, Kaveri, Mahanadi) — source in Deccan plateau, dry up in summer.

Chapter 19: Water Pollution

Water pollution means making water dirty and unsafe. Polluted water harms people, animals, and plants. Water is precious — protect it!

⚠️
⚠️ Alarming fact: Due to high levels of water pollution, aquatic animals face a greater threat of extinction than land animals!
Animated Pollution Diagram
Watch pollution flow into the river
Water Purification — Step by Step
Keeping Water Clean — Tips
🫙
Cover drinking water
Always cover vessels to prevent dust & insects falling in.
🔥
Boil water
Boiling kills germs and makes water safe to drink.
🔍
Use purifiers
Water purifiers filter out germs and impurities effectively.
🥄
Use a long-handled cup
Prevents fingers from touching stored drinking water.
❌
Don't dump waste
Never dump garbage or waste into rivers, ponds, or lakes.
🚜
Reduce pesticides
Excessive farm chemicals seep into rivers and groundwater.
📖 True Story — Plachimada, Kerala: Villagers discovered water levels dropped drastically. A soft-drink company was drawing millions of litres of underground water. They protested, went to court, and won! The factory was shut down — a great example of fighting for water rights.

Chapter 20: Animals for Transport

Ever since humans learnt to domesticate animals, they've been used for riding, pulling loads, and carrying goods across terrains.

🐘
📜 History: In ancient times, kings used horses for chariots and elephants in battles. The bullock cart is one of India's oldest means of transport!
